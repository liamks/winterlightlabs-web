Transcription and Analysis for Speech-Language Pathologists

We transcribe and analyze your recordings so you can see more patients.

Are you interested in participating in our free beta program?

What's involved?

  1. Administer a picture description task
  2. Record your patient's response
  3. Upload the recorded response to WinterLight Labs
  4. Receive a transcript & analysis (see sample below)
  5. Give us feedback!
